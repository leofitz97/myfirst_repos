This is a chicken recipes when is very delicious.

And i am going to through with you the steps to make it on your own.

Ingredients

  • One pound of chicken
  • 2 full Onions
  • Garlic about 3 medium size
  • Half of One ball of ginger
  • Half teaspoon of salt.
  • Two sachet of Gino tomato paste.

Steps:

  1. Put the saucepan on top of the oven and add a little water to it.
  2. Slice the chicken into how big or small you want and put it into the boiling water.
  3. Blend the Garlic and Ginger and pour it on the sliced chicken
  4. Wait for it to steam about 5 mins
  5. Blend the pepper and pour it on the steaming chicken.
  6. Wait for about 2 mins
